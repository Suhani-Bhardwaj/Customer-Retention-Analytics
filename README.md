# 📊 Customer Retention Analytics using Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer Retention Analytics is an end-to-end Business Intelligence project developed using **Power BI** to analyze customer retention, churn, loyalty program performance, promotional effectiveness, store performance, and Customer Lifetime Value (CLV).

The project transforms raw customer transaction data into interactive dashboards that help businesses understand customer behavior, identify retention opportunities, and make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Customer retention is one of the most important factors influencing long-term business growth. Businesses often struggle to identify why customers leave, which customer segments generate the highest value, and how loyalty programs or promotional campaigns affect customer engagement.

This project aims to answer questions such as:

- Which regions have the highest customer churn?
- Which loyalty tiers contribute the highest customer lifetime value?
- Do promotions improve customer engagement?
- Which sales channels perform better?
- Which customer segments should the business focus on?

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze customer retention and churn.
- Measure customer loyalty and repeat purchase behavior.
- Evaluate promotional campaign effectiveness.
- Compare online and store channel performance.
- Analyze Customer Lifetime Value (CLV).
- Identify high-value customer segments.
- Provide business recommendations based on data insights.

## 5️⃣ Executive Summary & Business Recommendations

![Executive Summary](Dashboard%20Screenshots/05_Executive_Summary.png)

### Executive Summary

This dashboard summarizes the overall business performance and highlights major findings from the analysis.

### Major Findings

- Repeat customers contribute significantly to long-term revenue.
- Loyalty engagement positively impacts Customer Lifetime Value.
- Promotional campaigns increase customer participation.
- Certain regions and store categories experience higher churn.

### Business Recommendations

- Improve retention strategies for lower loyalty tiers.
- Expand targeted promotional campaigns.
- Strengthen engagement in high-churn regions.
- Focus marketing efforts on high-value customer segments.
- Enhance loyalty rewards to encourage repeat purchases.
